Humidity

The humidity graph follows approximately the same trend as the graph of max temperature vs. latitude, albeit far less rigidly. There is a large cluster of locations with 100% humidity near the equator and places where the temperature is relatively high. This is data supports the fact that warm temperatures can hold more moisture in the air.
